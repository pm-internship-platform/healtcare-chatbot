Programs & Schemes

  1. This page contains Programs & Schemes carried out by Odisha Government

Empowering Lives Through Better Healthcare Access

Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY)

A nationwide health insurance program offering ₹5 lakh annual coverage per family for secondary and tertiary care. It benefits over 55 crore citizens with cashless treatment at empanelled hospitals.

Central Government Health Scheme (CGHS)

Provides all-round medical care—including allopathy and AYUSH—to central government employees, pensioners, and their dependents through wellness centres in major cities.

Pradhan Mantri Bharatiya Janaushadhi Pariyojana (PMBJP)

Ensures affordable generic medicines through Janaushadhi Kendras, expanding to 14,000+ outlets across India by 2026.

Pradhan Mantri Atmanirbhar Swasth Bharat Yojana (PMASBY)

Strengthens India’s healthcare infrastructure with a ₹64,180 crore investment for upgraded health facilities and diagnostics.

Universal Immunisation Programme

Provides free vaccines to children nationwide against diseases such as polio, measles, diphtheria, and tuberculosis.

Aarogya Setu App

A digital health advisory and contact-tracing app launched to fight COVID-19. It provides real-time alerts and guidance for citizens.

Ayushman Arogya Mandir

Upgraded Health & Wellness Centres offering maternal care, free medicines, diagnostics, and preventive health services.

AI-Driven Maternal & Child Health Pilot – Odisha

Uses AI-powered diagnostic kits for ASHA workers to monitor pregnancies and ensure timely healthcare in tribal regions.
